Watcard Information

Where to spend your money
  • Dine from a wide variety of cuisines available off campus that accept flex dollars! Our personal reccomendations are Domino's Pizza, Mongolian Grill, Mucho Burrito and Williams Fresh Cafe!
  • Use your WatCard and take advantage of the different services provided by our university like Athletics and Recreation, The Central store, The Chem store, WUSA locations in SLC, Housing and residences, Our libraries, Parking services, Registrar’s office, W store, W print, Student financial services and Laundry on campus!
  • Access the on campus Vending machines and dining locations our recommendations are CMH market and V1 Mudie's!
Healthy Choices
  • Daily Gluten free, Vegetarian/vegan, Halal, Dairy free options available at Mudie’s V1 and CMH market.
  • A wide variety of healthy options are available throughout campus. Our recommendations: Rolltation, Subway and FRSH Vegan.
Discount Information
Get the following discounts using your WatCard:
  • Get 10% off in all Bulk Barn locations on Wednesdays!
  • Get 10% off in Valu-Mart stores on Mondays, Tuesdays and Wednesdays!
  • Get 10% off in all Zehrs stores on Tuesdays!
  • Buy discounted movie tickets for Cineplex Cinemas at Conestoga Mall and Princess Cinemas in Uptown Waterloo.
  • Get food for 50% off the marked price in all 24 UW food services locations with a meal plan
  • If you’re an engineering student, bring your WatCard to the EngSoc office for a special sticker to get deals at DōShack, The Break Room, Crossroads Board Game Café, Al Madina, Sweet Dreams Teashop, Marble Slab Creamery, Baba Chicken Grill, and Mongolian Grill
Bike Storage Activiation
Your WatCard can also be used to pay for safely storing your bikes. Please contact the WatCard service center at SLC in person or via mail to activate this service.
